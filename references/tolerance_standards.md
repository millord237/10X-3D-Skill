# Glass Manufacturing Tolerance Standards

Industry-standard tolerances for glass fabrication based on ISO 12543 and ASTM C1036.

## Dimensional Tolerances

### Cut Size Tolerances

| Panel Size | Tolerance |
|------------|-----------|
| Up to 1000mm | +/- 1.0mm |
| 1001 - 2000mm | +/- 1.5mm |
| 2001 - 3000mm | +/- 2.0mm |
| Over 3000mm | +/- 2.5mm |

### Thickness Tolerances (Float Glass)

| Nominal Thickness | Tolerance |
|-------------------|-----------|
| 3mm | +/- 0.2mm |
| 4mm | +/- 0.2mm |
| 5mm | +/- 0.2mm |
| 6mm | +/- 0.2mm |
| 8mm | +/- 0.3mm |
| 10mm | +/- 0.3mm |
| 12mm | +/- 0.4mm |
| 15mm | +/- 0.5mm |
| 19mm | +/- 1.0mm |

## Hole Tolerances

### Position Tolerance

| Hole Diameter | Position Tolerance |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Up to 20mm | +/- 0.5mm |
| 21 - 50mm | +/- 1.0mm |
| 51 - 100mm | +/- 1.5mm |
| Over 100mm | +/- 2.0mm |

### Diameter Tolerance

| Nominal Diameter | Tolerance |
|------------------|-----------|
| Up to 20mm | +/- 0.5mm |
| 21 - 50mm | +/- 1.0mm |
| Over 50mm | +/- 1.5mm |

### Minimum Edge Distance

| Glass Thickness | Min Edge Distance |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| 3-6mm | 25mm or 2x thickness |
| 8-10mm | 30mm or 2x thickness |
| 12-19mm | 40mm or 2x thickness |

### Minimum Hole Spacing

- Center-to-center: >= 2x larger hole diameter
- Minimum: 50mm between hole edges

## Edge Quality Standards

### Seamed Edge
- Removes sharp edges only
- Width: 1-2mm
- No polish required

### Flat Polish
- Tolerance: +/- 0.5mm width
- Surface finish: 60 micro-inch or better
- No visible chips or scratches

### Pencil Polish
- Radius: Consistent within +/- 0.5mm
- Surface finish: Smooth, no flat spots

### Beveled Edge
- Angle tolerance: +/- 2 degrees
- Width tolerance: +/- 1mm

## Flatness Tolerances

### Annealed Glass
| Panel Size | Max Bow |
|------------|---------|
| Up to 1m | 3mm |
| 1-2m | 4mm |
| 2-3m | 5mm |

### Tempered Glass
- Roller wave: Max 0.3mm per 300mm
- Overall bow: 1.5x annealed tolerance

## Surface Quality

### Defect Limits (per square meter)
| Defect Type | Max Count | Max Size |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Scratches | 3 | 25mm length |
| Bubbles | 5 | 2mm diameter |
| Inclusions | 3 | 1mm diameter |
| Chips (edge) | 0 | N/A |

## Tempering Standards

### Heat Soak Test (HST)
- Temperature: 290C +/- 10C
- Duration: Minimum 2 hours
- Purpose: Reduces spontaneous breakage risk

### Break Pattern (Required for Safety)
- Minimum 40 particles per 50x50mm area
- No pieces larger than specified limit

## Quality Inspection

### Visual Inspection Distance
- Standard: 2 meters (6.5 feet)
- Viewing angle: Perpendicular to surface
- Lighting: Diffused daylight or equivalent

### Dimensional Verification
- Calibrated steel tape (tolerance: 0.1mm)
- Hole positions: From datum edges
- All critical dimensions checked
